HUNDREDS RECORDS and the music of Steve Peisch

Steve Peisch is a commissioned composer and songwriter, but he is best-know for his eponymously-named rock band from the 80s called the steves, which reflected his interest in the punk, metal, and minimalist music of that era. He was recently signed by Osmian Music/Captured Tracks, which will soon release his vinyl two vinyl 45RPM and five digital downloads from that period, including the song "Jerk!" described as "the best punk song every written." Peisch's most recent band, blue box set, opened for Andy Pratt, Jim Weider, Beatlejuice, and The Fools, and they were recorded by Brian Charles at zippah studios in Boston. Peisch's piano music has been performed and recorded by David Pihl, piano; his art songs have been performed and recorded by Charlotte Russell, soprano; and several of his compositions have been licensed through gettyimages.
